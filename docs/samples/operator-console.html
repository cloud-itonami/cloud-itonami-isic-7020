Action gate (Consulting Engagement Governor)

HARD holds cannot be overridden by a human approver. Professional-standards spec-basis, evidence completeness, engagement-scope containment and conflict-of-interest status are independently recomputed, never trusted from the advisor's proposal; a real deliverable issuance is always a human consultant's call, at every rollout phase.

OpGate
:engagement/intakephase-3 auto-commit when clean, no capital risk yet -- the ONLY auto-eligible op in this domain
:finding/researchALWAYS human approval · spec-basis independently checked against consulting.facts, never fabricated
:conflict/screenALWAYS human approval when clean · an unresolved conflict of interest is a HARD, un-overridable hold instead
:actuation/issue-deliverableALWAYS human approval · a real recommendation/deliverable issued to a client · evidence-completeness + engagement-scope-exceeded + double-issuance guards enforced, never auto at any phase
